Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download movies from Surya Movies?
To download movies, simply search for your desired movie, click on the download button, and choose your preferred quality. You'll need to create a free account to access download links.
Is Surya Movies completely free?
Yes, Surya Movies offers free access to our entire movie library. However, we do display ads to support our platform and cover operational costs.
Can I request movies that aren't available on the platform?
Absolutely! We value user feedback and regularly update our collection based on user requests. Simply use our request form to suggest movies you'd like to see.
What video qualities are available?
We offer movies in various qualities including 480p, 720p, 1080p, and even 4K for select titles. The available options depend on the source material.
Is it legal to watch movies on Surya Movies?
Surya Movies operates as a streaming aggregator and does not host any content on its servers. We simply provide links to content available on the internet. Users are responsible for ensuring they comply with their local copyright laws.
